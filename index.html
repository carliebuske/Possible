For TheBuffs

A college campus store in Boulder, CO, home of the CU Buffaloes, asked me to create some graphics & designs to print on hats, shirts, & other collegiate gear. The sticker culture in Boulder, CO is very substantial - it's a funky way decorate the city while also served as a marketing strategy for local businesses and products.
